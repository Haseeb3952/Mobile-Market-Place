Privacy Policy

Welcome to Mobile Market Place! Your privacy is important to us, and we want you to feel comfortable using our website and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase from us.

Information We Collect

When you visit Mobile Market Place, we may collect various types of information, including:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, username, and password.
  • Listing Information: When you list an item for sale, we collect information about the item, such as its description, price, and condition. We may also collect photos of the item.
  • Transaction Information: When you buy or sell an item on the Website, we collect information about the transaction, such as the item price, shipping costs, and payment method.

We may disclose your information to the following third parties:

Your Choices
